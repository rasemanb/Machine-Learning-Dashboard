Analysis

For our Machine Learning part of the project, we decided to try and predict the average rating of a specific genre using neural networks trained on average user rating with almost 270,000 ratings and 21 different genres. This model was trained on the Action genre and had 2 hidden layers (4 layers total). Our model was able to predict the average user rating of a certain genre with an accuracy score of 0.9892 and an MSE of 0.0634, meaning it would predict the rating within about 0.06 points.

Our second model was trained on a specific title of a movie and attempted to predict the average user rating. The data contained over 2500 users and almost 5000 titles. Our model was trained on the movie Legend, and had 2 hidden layers as well (4 layers total).
